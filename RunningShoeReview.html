Running Shoe Reviews

Welcome to our blog dedicated to reviewing the newest technology, trends, and releases of running shoes. We welcome all experience levels and perspectives!

Written By Runners, For Runners

We are a team of running enthusiasts, volunteers, and coaches who want to make sure you find the best shoe. This blog will cover the basics of picking a shoe, care, and when it's inevitably time to find your new pair.

We'll explore the best (and worst) brands on the market and give you transparent and thoughful reviews of shoes for all runners at all levels. Welcome!

Notice of Transparency

Our contributors do not receive payment for their work. This is a labor of love, much like running! Sometimes brands send us samples to test, but we will always disclose this information and we will never receive a portion of any sales. We hope that this site is informative, inclusive, and community-focused.
