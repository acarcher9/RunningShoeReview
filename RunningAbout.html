About Us

We are a group of avid runners of all ages to contribute reviews, blog posts, and conversation. We aim to inform others about the importance of running in good shoes! Ensuring proper fit for your body and running style can promote prolonged joint health by preventing injury and muscle pain. We also keep an eye on the latest technology of shoe design. Our site is open for added contributors so do not heistate to contact us to join the team!

Origins

Founded in 2005 by two sisters, our blog was originated by Annie and Emily while they trained for their first marathon! They discovered that they needed help picking out shoes as they trained, but they struggled to find a community of runners who could discuss their favorite brands, pros and cons, and experiences without influence from companies.
